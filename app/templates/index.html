My homage to the NYT Spelling Bee game


An absolutely brilliant and addictive word game that I play everyday!

Thank you to The New York Times. Click here for the official game


Official NYT Rules:

Create words using letters from the hive.
  • Words must contain at least 4 letters.
  • Words must include the center letter.
  • Our word list does not include words that are obscure, hyphenated, or proper nouns.
  • No cussing either, sorry.
  • Letters can be used more than once.

Gameplay Differences in this Version:

The rules above still apply, but...
  • I use a different dictionary: 12Dicts.
  • You can play any number of games in a day!
